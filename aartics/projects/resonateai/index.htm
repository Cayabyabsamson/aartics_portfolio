Market Research

Competitive and Comparative Analysis

Studied current competitors, inline text editors and document composers. Also studied MailChimp's product voice and tone guide.

Outcomes: Cleaner visualization of an inline text editor, idea of delightful interaction including clear tonality guide, and Key features needed for the product.

Personas


Developed key personas for members of a financial startup and identified their habits, goals, and needs.

User testing with paper prototype


Quick paper prototype was used in early usability testing, leading to identification of new features and clear prioritization among them.

This also led us to research on simplifying the tonality.

Restructuring tonality:


User research led us to remove non measurable tones like "joy", "anticipation", and "popularity" from the previous list. Simplified the classes, classified it under 'Persuation' and 'Moments'.

Final product scored users on "Trust", "Confidence", and "Likability", leading to better resonance with the audience.
