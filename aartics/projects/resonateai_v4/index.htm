resonate.ai

As a UX Designer for Resonate.ai, I helped define an experience of how machine learning and A.I. can aid users sound more trustworthy and confident

Team size: 3
Duration: 4 weeks

problem

Our client Resonate.ai approached us with a machine learning algorithm that can suggest the "tone" of any content. Our challenge was to design a delightful experience for financial marketers to use the product.

problem synthesis

usability testing

Usability testing on existing product led us to remove non measurable tones like "joy", "anticipation", and "popularity" from the existing product classifiers. We priortized the features to:

  • 1. Reduce classifiers: Identify and Infuse Persuasion in inline content
  • 2. Data Mining: Identify structural elements in overall content
  • 3. Brand customization: Train the algorithm to identify and infuse brand personalization in inline content
