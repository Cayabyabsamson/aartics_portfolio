karuna.vr

As a UX Engineer intern, I helped to create the navigation menu scene for a Virtual Reality application for pain management

Team size: 1
Duration: 6 months

problem

Our client Resonate.ai approached us with a machine learning algorithm that can suggest the "tone" of any content. Our challenge was to design a delightful experience for financial marketers to use the product.

solution

We designed and delivered prototypes for an application which can make tone suggestions in a web-based document editor and a plug-in for email applications. The feel, voice and tone for the product attempts to mimic Scarlett Johanssen's movie personalities.

process
