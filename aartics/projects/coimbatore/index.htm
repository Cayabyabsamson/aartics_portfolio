Project Context

Role: Architect, Arris Architects, Mumbai

Design Communication in Plans


The plans for the new mall conveyed the approaches, frontages, multiplex, open-to-air courts, and marked all the profitable leasable area to the clients. The also serve as an important structural design document

3D Views of the Design Concept


I conducted several rapid design iterations with computer generated 3D models, to be able to present several design options. The final design aesthetic is inspired by forms that are reminiscent of the local motifs such as the delicate textiles and the tall palm trees. We also innovated on the planning, by proposed a semi-open air mall for climatic benefit.
